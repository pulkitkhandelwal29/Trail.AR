How It Works

3 Easy Steps

Scan the Marker
  • Using your smartphone or tablet, open the QR code scanner app. (comes by default in mobiles)
  • Point your device's camera at the QR code marker placed within the indoor environment and scan it.
  • Instantly, you're redirected to the website within your device's browser, where the indoor navigation experience begins.
Select Your Destination
  • Upon landing on the website, a dropdown menu appears on the screen, presenting a list of nearby destinations within the building.
  • Browse through the list and select your desired destination from the dropdown menu, whether it's a specific room, facility, or point of interest.
Start Navigation
  • Once you've selected your destination, the navigation system calculates the most optimal route from your current location to the chosen destination.
  • AR's intuitive directions will overlay on your device's screen, guiding you step-by-step through the indoor environment.
  • Follow the directions displayed on your screen to navigate effortlessly, all within your device's browser, without the need for any additional app installations.
